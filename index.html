What is Bubble Shot?

This startup aims at raising awerness about drug issues in parties. The concept is simple: you can have a shot of alcohol (Vodka, Tequila, Gin), but trapped inside a bubble. To drink it, you only have to put it in your mouth and make it explodes! With this shape, it is impossible to get drugged. Plus, it changes your consumong habits because it is more fun!
